Prime Children's Hospital

Project Overview

Prime Children's Hospital located on Mini-Bypass Road, Nellore, is a state-of-the-art healthcare facility constructed by Syam Infra. The project involved complex structural engineering to support medical equipment and provide a safe, sterile environment for patients and medical staff.


Our team ensured that all MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) systems were designed and executed to the highest standards, ensuring uninterrupted services for the hospital.
